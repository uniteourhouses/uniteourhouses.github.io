Winter  Is  Coming

Iowa winters are cold. Bring a jacket or your warmest fur. Dress attire is Hobbit Formal. We'll let you know when we know what that means.

Settle down with us for a night of feasting, drinking, laughing, dancing, a toasty fire, and the loving companionship of friends and family.

Your Presence is Requested

We understand that not every guest will be able join us for this special occasion. Braving an Iowa winter is not a journey for the weak hearted...

You won't find a registry because your presence is more than enough of a gift. If you are able to stay for the after dinner feastivities you will be helping to start the next chapter in our story.

Seriously, do not bring a gift. We have to take everything back to Virgina. Cards are welcome if you absolutely feel the need.
